PRE-REVENUE
No revenue or paying customers yet
Product is demo-ready but hasn’t been deployed with live engineering teams generating real usage data.
Free pilot tier designed for fast activation. Targeting 5 design partners in first 90 days post-raise to validate retention and pricing.

Dependent on third-party AI models

Enterprise tier includes self-hosted/VPC deployment. SOC 2 compliance on roadmap. Data stays within customer environment for Enterprise customers.
